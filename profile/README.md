
# With Rajesh

> Building technology that helps people connect, learn, build, collaborate, and grow.

## 🚀 About

With Rajesh is a technology and innovation initiative focused on creating practical solutions through software, artificial intelligence, education, and open collaboration.

We build products, platforms, and ecosystems that empower individuals, startups, organizations, and communities to achieve more through technology.

## 🛠️ What We Build

- AI-powered applications and agents
- Open source software and developer tools
- Learning and knowledge platforms
- Productivity and business solutions
- Community and collaboration ecosystems
- Digital transformation initiatives

## 🌟 Our Principles

- Open Collaboration
- Continuous Learning
- Practical Innovation
- Community First
- Long-Term Impact

## 🎯 Vision

To create an ecosystem where anyone can learn, create, contribute, collaborate, and grow through technology.

## 🤝 Contribute

We welcome developers, designers, creators, educators, researchers, startups, and organizations to collaborate on open source projects and innovative initiatives.

Whether you're fixing a bug, improving documentation, sharing ideas, or building something new, your contribution matters.
